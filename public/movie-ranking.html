Top 3 Movies of all-times

The Thing

John Carpenter's The Thing is a 1982 science fiction horror film that has become a cult classic. The film follows a group of researchers in Antarctica who encounter a shape-shifting alien that can imitate any living organism. The film is known for its practical effects, suspenseful atmosphere, and themes of paranoia and isolation.

The Shinning

Stanley Kubrick's The Shining is a 1980 horror film based on Stephen King's novel of the same name. The film follows a family that becomes isolated in a haunted hotel during the winter. The film is known for its unsettling atmosphere, iconic performances, and themes of madness and isolation.

Thor: Ragnarok

Thor: Ragnarok is a 2017 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Thor. The film follows Thor as he teams up with Hulk and Valkyrie to save Asgard from the villain Hela. The film is known for its humor, colorful visuals, and action-packed sequences.
